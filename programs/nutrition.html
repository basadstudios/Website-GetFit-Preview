Program Structure

Your 12-Week Journey

Our comprehensive program guides you through three phases to transform your nutrition habits and health.

Weeks 1-4: Foundation & Assessment

Build the groundwork for your nutrition transformation through assessment and education.

  • Comprehensive nutrition assessment

  • Initial body composition analysis

  • Food journal analysis

  • Establishing baseline nutritional needs

  • Nutrition fundamentals education

Weeks 5-8: Implementation & Adjustment

Put your personalized nutrition plan into action with guided implementation and regular fine-tuning.

  • Personalized meal plan implementation

  • Weekly grocery lists and recipes

  • Bi-weekly check-ins and adjustments

  • Addressing challenges and obstacles

  • Progress measurements

Weeks 9-12: Optimization & Long-term Strategy

Fine-tune your nutrition plan and develop strategies for sustainable long-term success.

  • Advanced nutrition strategies

  • Meal prep mastery workshops

  • Special situations planning (travel, social events)

  • Final assessment and progress review

  • Long-term nutrition maintenance plan
