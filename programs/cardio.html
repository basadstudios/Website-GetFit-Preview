Program Structure

Your 8-Week Journey

Our program gradually builds cardiovascular endurance and efficiency while preventing burnout and overtraining.

Phase 1: Foundation

Weeks 1-2

Establish baseline cardiovascular fitness and introduce key training methods with moderate intensity workouts.

  • Fitness assessment

  • Introduction to heart rate zones

  • 3-4 cardio sessions per week

  • Building aerobic base

Phase 2: Development

Weeks 3-5

Progressively increase workout duration and intensity while introducing varied cardio modalities for balanced improvement.

  • Interval training introduction

  • 4-5 cardio sessions per week

  • Mixed modality training

  • Increased duration

Phase 3: Optimization

Weeks 6-8

Focus on specific adaptations based on your goals with more specialized and challenging cardio workouts.

  • High-intensity intervals

  • Endurance-focused sessions

  • Performance testing

  • Peak cardiovascular conditioning

Ongoing Support

Post-Program

After completing the 8-week program, receive a maintenance plan to continue progress or prepare for your next goal.

  • Maintenance plan

  • Progress review

  • Goal reassessment

  • Advanced options available
