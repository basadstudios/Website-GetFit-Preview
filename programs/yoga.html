Practice Variety

Explore Various Yoga Styles

Our program incorporates multiple yoga styles to provide a well-rounded practice that addresses all aspects of physical and mental wellbeing.

Hatha Yoga

A gentle introduction to basic yoga poses, perfect for beginners learning the foundations of yoga practice.

Key Benefits: Improved flexibility, stress reduction, foundation building

Vinyasa Flow

A dynamic practice that links movement with breath, creating a flowing sequence of poses that builds strength and endurance.

Key Benefits: Cardiovascular health, strength building, mind-body connection

Yin Yoga

A slow-paced style where poses are held for longer periods to target deep connective tissues and increase flexibility.

Key Benefits: Deep flexibility, joint health, stress relief, meditation

Restorative Yoga

Ultra-relaxing practice using props to support the body in gentle poses for extended periods, promoting deep relaxation.

Key Benefits: Stress reduction, nervous system regulation, recovery

Program Structure

Your 6-Week Journey

Our program progressively builds your practice and mindfulness skills week by week.

Week 1-2: Foundation

Establish the fundamentals of yoga practice with proper alignment, breathing techniques, and basic poses.

  • Introduction to yoga philosophy

  • Basic asana practice

  • Breath awareness

  • Foundational alignment principles

Week 3-4: Development

Build upon the foundation with more challenging poses and deeper breathwork practices.

  • Intermediate poses

  • Flow sequences

  • Pranayama techniques

  • Guided meditation practice

Week 5-6: Integration

Connect all elements of the practice while exploring more advanced techniques and longer holds.

  • Advanced variations

  • Longer sequences

  • Deep relaxation

  • Mind-body integration
