Program Structure

Your 12-Week Journey

Our program is structured to progressively build strength and muscle while preventing plateaus and overtraining.

Phase 1: Foundation

Weeks 1-4

Focus on proper form, establishing baseline strength, and building a solid foundation with moderate weights and higher repetitions.

  • Form assessment

  • 3 training days per week

  • Full-body workouts

  • Foundational movement patterns

Phase 2: Development

Weeks 5-8

Increase training volume and intensity with more specialized split routines to target specific muscle groups for growth.

  • 4 training days per week

  • Upper/lower body split

  • Progressive overload focus

  • Accessory exercises introduction

Phase 3: Advancement

Weeks 9-12

Fine-tune your training with advanced techniques to break through plateaus and maximize strength and muscle gains.

  • 4-5 training days per week

  • Specialized training splits

  • Advanced lifting techniques

  • Peak strength development
